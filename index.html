What We Do

Signet Metals Ltd is a UK-registered scrap metal trading company specialising in the export of ferrous and non-ferrous metals to international markets.

We operate across the UK–South Asia trade corridor, connecting quality-verified UK scrap supply with established buyers. Our focus is on building long-term partnerships grounded in transparency, grade consistency, and reliable logistics.

Ferrous Metals

HMS grades, shredded steel, heavy melting scrap, and alloy steels — sourced from accredited UK suppliers and graded to ISRI specifications.

Non-Ferrous Metals

Aluminium, copper, stainless steel, and specialty alloys. Expanding our product range to serve diverse industrial requirements.

Logistics & Compliance

End-to-end export management including containerisation, documentation, customs clearance, and full regulatory compliance with UK waste export regulations.
